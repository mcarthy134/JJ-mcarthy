About JJ McCarthy

JJ McCarthy is an American football quarterback known for his poise, accuracy, and leadership on the field. Born on January 20, 2003, in La Grange Park, Illinois, he rose to national prominence as the quarterback of the University of Michigan Wolverines, leading them to the College Football Playoff National Championship.
